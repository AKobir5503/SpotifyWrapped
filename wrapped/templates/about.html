What is SpotifyWrapped?

SpotifyWrapped is your personalized music companion, designed to provide a detailed summary of your Spotify listening habits. From your most-played tracks and favorite artists to unique insights about your music taste, we bring your musical journey to life in a fun and interactive way.

With SpotifyWrapped, you can explore your top genres, relive your favorite moments, and share your music profile with friends and family!

Features

SpotifyWrapped offers an array of features to make your experience memorable:

  • Top Tracks & Artists: Discover your most-streamed songs and favorite performers.
  • Listening Trends: See how your music taste evolves over time.
  • Genre Breakdown: Dive deep into the genres that shape your playlist.
  • Interactive Visuals: Enjoy colorful and creative slides that highlight your music journey.
  • Shareable Insights: Share your SpotifyWrapped with friends and make your music profile social!
